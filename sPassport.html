Application Procedure for Standard Passport

Requirements for obtaining Standard Passport

The standard passport can be obtained with any of the following reasons:

  1. Fresh (First) Application
  2. Re-Issuance of Passport

Fresh Passport Application and Re-Issuance of Passport Requirements visit the immigration website here
